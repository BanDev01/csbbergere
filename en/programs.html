Our teaching programs

La Bonne Bergère School Complex offers structured teaching adapted to each school level, to provide a solid education that helps students develop their knowledge, skills and values.

Photo: Kindergarten section
Level 1

Kindergarten

The kindergarten section welcomes young children in a warm, secure environment that supports their overall development.

At this level, learning mainly happens through:

  • Educational and playful activities
  • Language development
  • An introduction to reading and writing
  • Educational games
  • The development of creativity and social skills

The goal is to prepare children to gradually enter formal schooling.

Level 2

Primary

The primary level forms the foundation of students' academic education.

Teaching mainly covers:

  • Reading and writing
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Civic and moral education
  • Cultural and educational activities

This level allows students to acquire fundamental knowledge and develop their capacity for reflection and work.

Photo: Primary section
Photo: Secondary section
Level 3

Secondary

The secondary level builds on the knowledge acquired in primary school and prepares students for official exams as well as further studies.

Students develop:

  • Their analytical skills
  • Their independence in their studies
  • Their sense of responsibility
  • Their readiness for their academic and professional future
Our teaching methods

Teaching methods that support student success

At La Bonne Bergère School Complex, we apply teaching methods that support our students' success.

Structured teaching

Structured, progressive teaching, adapted to each student's pace.

Rigorous supervision

Rigorous discipline and regular monitoring of students' progress.

Active participation

Active participation from learners and complementary educational activities.

These methods allow students to learn in a serious, motivating environment, while developing their full potential.
