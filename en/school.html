Our school

History, vision, mission, values and a message from the leadership of La Bonne Bergère School Complex.

History

A school serving the community

La Bonne Bergère School Complex was founded with the ambition of offering children a quality education in a disciplined environment conducive to learning.

Since its founding, the school has shaped responsible, diligent students capable of succeeding in their studies. Thanks to the commitment of its leadership and teachers, the institution has steadily grown to meet the educational needs of the community.

Today, La Bonne Bergère School Complex continues its mission of supporting students in their intellectual, moral and social development.

Photo: The school over time
Vision

Becoming a leading school

The vision of La Bonne Bergère School Complex is to become a leading school in education, recognized for the quality of its teaching, the discipline of its students and the excellence of its academic results.

The school aims to shape young people capable of contributing positively to the development of society through their knowledge, skills and values.

Mission

Providing a complete education

The mission of La Bonne Bergère School Complex is to provide a complete education that allows students to develop their intellectual, moral and social abilities.

The school is committed to:

  • Ensuring quality teaching
  • Encouraging hard work and perseverance
  • Developing a sense of responsibility
  • Preparing students to succeed in their academic and professional future
Values

The core values that guide our actions

Discipline

We encourage respect for rules and a strong sense of responsibility.

Hard work

We value effort, perseverance and commitment to one's studies.

Excellence

We aim for the best academic results and the full development of every student's potential.

Respect

We foster a school climate based on mutual respect between students, teachers and parents.

Message from the leadership
"

At La Bonne Bergère School Complex, we believe every child has unique potential that deserves to be nurtured.

Our mission is to support students throughout their schooling by offering serious supervision, quality teaching and an environment conducive to their success.

We invite parents to trust us with their children's education, so that together we can build a future founded on discipline, hard work and excellence.
