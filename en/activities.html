Our school activities

At La Bonne Bergère School Complex, education is not limited to classroom lessons. We regularly organize educational and cultural activities that help students develop their talents, creativity and team spirit.

Photo: Career Day
Activity 1

Career Day

Career Day is an educational activity that lets students discover different trades and professions.

Through this activity, students can:

  • Present a profession
  • Understand the role of different professions in society
  • Develop their imagination and self-confidence

This activity also helps students think about their future career path.

Activity 2

Cultural activities

Cultural activities play an important role in students' development.

They help develop:

  • Creativity
  • Artistic expression
  • A sense of collaboration

These activities include:

  • Singing and dancing
  • Arts and crafts
  • Cultural events organized by the school
Photo: Cultural activities
Photo: Educational field trips
Activity 3

Educational field trips

La Bonne Bergère School Complex also organizes educational field trips so students can learn in different ways, outside the classroom.

These trips give students the opportunity to:

  • Discover new environments
  • Observe the reality of certain fields of activity
  • Broaden their knowledge
Activity 4

Student projects

Students regularly take part in various educational projects that let them put into practice the knowledge learned in class.

These projects encourage:

  • Research
  • Creativity
  • Teamwork
  • Taking initiative

These activities help shape active students who are engaged in their own learning.
